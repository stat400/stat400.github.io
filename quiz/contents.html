General Guidelines

  • You may either give an exact answer (the relative tolerance is 1e-5), or round to 4 decimal places. Fractions (not expressions) are accepted. These are largely the same guidelines for homework and exams. (We may be more generous on HW and exams.)
  • The solutions tab will often not contain the numerical solution, or even many numbers at all. It instead guides you to find the answer. In either case, it should be used as a last resort.
  • If you believe there is a mistake with any question or solution, please bring it up in the Discord server.
  • Using calculators and/or R, and discussing questions with others are all permitted (and even encouraged!). On the other hand, be wary of chatbot use ‚Äî it might lead you in the right direction, but will give you the incorrect answer the vast majority of the time.
